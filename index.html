How to Read a Poem

A two-hour workshop. Diverse Intelligences Summer Institute. Every text, recording, and exercise is here.

Hour 1
  • Sweeney Agonistes — read the opener cold, then hear Eliot.
  • The point — reading as explicit and tacit skill; the AI-poetry study.
  • Prosody — Greek and Latin, Old English, Chaucer, Shakespeare.
  • Verse forms — the three sonnets, and forms across the centuries.
  • Scansion — the method, then scan for yourself; the room compares on the board.
Hour 2

The Waste Land, read aloud.
