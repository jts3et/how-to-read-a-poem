How to Read a Poem

A two-hour workshop. Diverse Intelligences Summer Institute. Every text, recording, and exercise is here.

Hour 1
  • Scan & submit — the opener: everyone scans a line, and the room’s disagreement lands live on the board.
  • Versification — how English verse was made: meter, form, and genre from Homer to free verse.
  • Breath — why the feet are what they are: meter as the shape of the breath.
  • Glossary — the terms, for reference.
Hour 2

The Waste Land, read aloud.
